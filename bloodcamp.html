Introduction & History

Started: June 2009

License received on 03.06.2009 (renewed in 2014).

Licenses & Camps:

  • Camp License: Dec 2011 — 35 camps conducted.
  • Component preparation license: 13.02.2013 — renewed till 02.06.2019

The Blood Bank caters to patients from Trichy and surrounding districts, ensuring quality service at affordable cost.

Components Prepared

  • Packed Red Blood Cells (PRBC)
  • Fresh Frozen Plasma (FFP)
  • Platelet Concentrate
  • Cryo Precipitate

Staff Training

Officers & technicians trained at Tamil Nadu Dr. MGR Medical University — 15 days basic training + 7 days component separation.

Objectives

  • Issue the right blood at the right time.
  • Prevention of transfusion reactions.
  • Proper disposal of expired & infected units.
  • Universal safety precautions.
