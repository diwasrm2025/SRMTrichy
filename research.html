Frequently Asked Questions

Answers about research and innovation at Trichy SRM Medical College.

Students can participate in research by approaching faculty members with research interests aligned with their own, joining the student research club, or applying for research internships through our Research Cell. We encourage undergraduate and postgraduate students to engage in research activities throughout their academic journey.

Our institution houses state-of-the-art research laboratories including molecular biology labs, advanced imaging facilities, animal research facilities, clinical trial units, and specialized equipment for various research domains. All facilities are equipped with modern technology and maintained to international standards.

Yes, we provide internal research grants for faculty and students. Additionally, we assist researchers in applying for external funding from agencies like ICMR, DBT, DST, and international funding bodies. Our Research Cell provides guidance on grant writing and project proposal development.

All research proposals involving human participants or animals must be submitted to our Institutional Ethics Committee. The committee meets monthly to review proposals, ensuring compliance with ethical guidelines, patient safety, and informed consent procedures. Approval is mandatory before initiating any research study.

We actively support researchers in publishing their work in peer-reviewed international and national journals. Our institution provides access to research databases, writing workshops, and mentorship for manuscript preparation. We also organize annual research conferences where students and faculty can present their findings.

Absolutely! We encourage and facilitate international collaborations. Our institution has active partnerships with renowned universities and research centers worldwide. Faculty members can apply for exchange programs, joint research projects, and collaborative grants through our International Relations Office.
