UG Courses

Undergraduate medical program (MBBS).

Description

The Undergraduate (UG) medical program provides foundational training in medicine, covering basic sciences, clinical skills, and patient care. It prepares students for a career as medical professionals through structured academics and hands-on clinical exposure.

PG Courses

Postgraduate medical programs (MD/MS/DNB).

Description

The Postgraduate (PG) medical programs offer advanced specialized training in various medical disciplines. These programs include MD (Doctor of Medicine), MS (Master of Surgery), and DNB (Diplomate of National Board), focusing on research, clinical excellence, and specialization in chosen fields.
